WELCOME GANLEE TO EXECUTOR CREATIVES

AUGUST 13, 2023

When we signed G2 Hel, we wanted to make sure we got the best roster available to compete at the highest level. After securing top-tier talent,

all we needed was a coach to bring the best out of the team.


With experience in both the LEC and a variety of regional leagues, we're proud to announce Finlay “Quaye” Stewart as our head coach for G2

Hel. We're confident that with Quaye's expertise in drafting and making the team gel, Hel will be set up for success in all future tournaments.


With their first trophy under their belt, we're excited to see Quaye support the team and build Hel's dynasty one step at a time.
